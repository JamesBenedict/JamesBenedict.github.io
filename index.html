Indiana University School of Journalism

D3 Selector Chart

Web Development

The final project for my second web design class at IU was to develop an interactive chart showing the possible dangerous levels of Acetampinophen when you combine certain medication. We were shown a propublica graphic as our model to recreate but all started from scratch. I decided to try out a very basic d3 chart selector and bar chart.
