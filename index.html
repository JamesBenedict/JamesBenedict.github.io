About

We should probably get this out of the way, if you call me with an offer I might respond by mimicking an airhorn. During the interview if you get me talking about something I like you might need me to slow down to understand every word. I'm an excitable person who isn't afraid to express my passions.

I've never been good at staying satisfied with a working understanding of my technology. I like that it is constantly updating and there is always more to learn. I must have given myself a dozen heart attacks jailbreaking my phone or playing with my computer's BIOS, but I love the rush of discovery. I promise if you let me into your system I won't break anything but expect plenty of question about how it works.

While tech fulfills my experimental interests, my passion is to bring more accountability into our society. Creating clear journalism has never been more important than today with the amount of misinformation spreading and memory holes forming. I could have been a cynic but chose to be a journalist. The best way to hold institutions accountable is through independent review and a hard look at their data. The best way to incite change is by telling clear and compelling stories that make the public reevaluate their positions.

I'm a quick learner who has told award winning stories with photos, layout, graphics and words and want to further my education producing data driven visuals in the professional world.
