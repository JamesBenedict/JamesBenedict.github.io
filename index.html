As a digital director I was faced with a staff unfamiliar with web development basics. I was able to address this by adapting my design from Waiting for Help into a user friendly template. Indiana Daily Student

Coverage of Hannah Wilson's murder

Breaking News Editing • Analytics Tracking • Photography • Videography

After IU senior Hannah Wilson was murdered, Bloomington was put in a national spotlight. The IDS's coverage was getting good traffic, but performing poorly on search engines. Additionally, I noticed, many users refreshing the original article looking for an update instead of viewing our latest one published. I created this collections page with links to all articles and multimedia surrounding Wilson's murder as they broke. I put the link on the front page and social media which generated an extra 60,000 views for our website.
