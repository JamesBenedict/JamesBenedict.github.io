Indiana Daily Student

Responsive Article Template
Developer • Art Director

As a digital director I was faced with a staff unfamiliar with web development basics. I was able to address this by adapting my design from Waiting for Help into a user friendly template. I passionately believe in the power of design to make the news more accessible and wanted to make it easier for print designer to start designing online. I then gave the template to faculty at IU’s new Media School, who are now using it in their curriculum for the most advanced multimedia course.

Indiana Daily Student

Waiting for Help

Web Development • Art Direction • Reporting

As the Digital Editor at the IDS, I helped create a custom website outside of our content management system for one of our larger stories about the counseling service on campus. I also researched the employment and budget records of all Big 10 Universities with help from Megan Jula the reporter. The site used the bootstrap framework for responsive design and highcharts.js for interactive graphics.
